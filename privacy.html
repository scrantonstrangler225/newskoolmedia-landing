Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

1. Introduction

New Skool Media (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is a sports media platform that allows athletes, podcasters, and organizations to create, share, and monetize sports content. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with applicable laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), where applicable.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal and usage information:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, username, password, and profile information
  • Monetization Data: Payment details (e.g., PayPal, Stripe), NIL sponsorship info, transaction history
  • Media Content: Uploaded videos, photos, audio, and metadata (e.g., tags, titles, locations)
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, device ID, cookies, and usage logs
  • Third-Party Integrations: If you link services (e.g., YouTube, TikTok, Spotify), we may collect tokens and activity data
  • Minor Consent Data: If you upload content featuring minors, we may collect digital signatures or documents proving guardian consent

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain your account
  • To facilitate media uploads, streaming, discovery, and monetization
  • To ensure legal compliance with NIL, DMCA, and child privacy laws
  • To process payments and sponsorships
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To improve and personalize the platform experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with the following parties:

  • Payment processors for managing NIL-related earnings
  • Analytics providers to improve platform performance
  • League or school partners when required by media rights agreements or to confirm eligibility
  • Legal authorities if required by law, subpoena, or to protect our users or the platform

5. Content Featuring Minors

If you upload content that includes individuals under the age of 18, you must ensure appropriate consent has been obtained from a parent or legal guardian. We reserve the right to request proof of consent or remove content that does not comply.

New Skool Media implements safeguards (such as age tagging, moderation, and limited monetization options) for minor-related content in compliance with COPPA and similar laws.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)
  • Request data portability
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at Contact Us.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, understand usage patterns, and improve platform performance. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse some or all cookies. Some features may not work properly without them.

8. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is 100% secure, and you acknowledge and accept some risk inherent to internet-based platforms.

9. Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including legal and operational obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to verification and exceptions required by law).

10. DMCA and Copyright

If you believe your copyrighted work has been uploaded to New Skool Media without authorization, please contact us at Contact Legal to file a takedown request. Include your contact info, the copyrighted material, and the location of the allegedly infringing content.

11. International Users

If you are accessing New Skool Media from outside the United States, you consent to the processing of your information in the U.S. and any other country where our servers are located. For EU users, our processing of personal data complies with the GDPR.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes occur, we will notify users via email or platform alert. The latest version will always be available on this page.
